Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 12, 2026

Welcome to Resume Analysis AI+. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your details when you upload resumes, write cover letters, or consult our AI career advisor.

1. Information We Collect

When you register an account or upload documents, we collect:

  • Account Credentials: Email address, passwords, and name.
  • Resume Data: PDF/DOCX files containing your work history, skills, contact information, and academic background.
  • Chat History & Scans: Conversations with the AI Career Advisor and ATS feedback query responses.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your details are processed to power our AI services. We utilize the information to:

  • Parse resume structures and calculate ATS optimization scores.
  • Generate contextual interview tips and cover letters.
  • Communicate with the Google Gemini API to process prompts securely.

3. Data Security and Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We send text segments from your resumes to Google's API to construct predictions and AI responses. All API keys and connections utilize secure SSL channels.

4. Your Control and Deletion Rights

You can delete your uploaded resumes or version histories at any time from the Candidate Workspace dashboard, which immediately removes them from our active database. To request complete account deletion, please submit a request via our Contact Support channel.
